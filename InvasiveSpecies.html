Description

This is an app that allows users to report sightings of invasive plant species in the Berwyn Heights area. In this app users are able to identify the invasive species in their area and create a report containing information about it. Once the report is created users other users can view it and mark the report as completed once the invasive plant specie is taken care of. This App was developed by Jordan Foster, Munachismo Ogochukwu, and Hamza Siddiqui
